About Us

Oliver’s Place is named after my spunky Jack Russel/ Chihuahua, Oliver. I adopted Oliver from my best friend in college and since then, I’ve never felt unsafe in his presence. Oliver’s Place takes that same mentality and applies it to your pets experience when they’re with us. We ensure that your cats and dogs feel safe and loved when they visit. At Oliver’s Place, we love all beings wether they walk on two legs or 4! And that’s why we’re proud to be your pets home away from home.

We know that your pet is more than a furry friend, they're family! That's why we only work with our trusted partners! We're proud to name Pawsome Foods, Furry Furniture Store, and Wagging Tails Housekeeping as our team mates! We work together to make sure your pet's stay is the cat's meow!

At Oliver’s Place, the safety of your pets is imperative. In order to keep your furry friend safe, we have breed and personality based accommodations available! Does Fido love to run all day, everyday? Every location has over 2 miles of greenery and play areas for your active pal! Kitty prefers alone time? We have both individual and shared rooms for all of our pets! And have no fear, specific diet accommodations are here! Sensitive tummies can breathe a collective sigh of relief. If there’s anything else we can do for you and your pawsome pal, just let us know at the time of reservation! It’s that easy!

Incredible care doesn’t follow a clock. That’s why Oliver’s Place is proud to have a team of dedicated pet lovers who understand this! Every caregiver is ready to make your pet comfy and is aware of your pets needs from a provided folder. We also have on call veterinarians in the case of any health emergencies. Petting, playtime, and general checking in is done every 15 - 30 minutes to ensure your pet never goes without.
